Famous tmux bars — for comparison

Eleven of the most popular tmux bars in the wild, pulled from their own README screenshots. Below them: your three approved designs (Glacier · Atelier · Bauhaus) so you can see how they compare. Most of the famous ones are powerline cascades — your set covers more structural ground.

Catppuccin tmux

by catppuccin/tmux
rounded pills · pastel
Soft pastel theme with rounded module separators in four flavors (Mocha, Macchiato, Frappé, Latte). Modular pill-style segments for session/window/metadata. The official catppuccin look — closest in spirit to your Atelier.

Oh My Tmux! (.tmux)

by gpakosz
classic powerline
The "default-ish" tmux look you see everywhere — arrow separators, hostname, uptime, battery, date/time on the right. The framework you're already using as ohmytmux base.

tmux2k

by 2KAbhishek
powerline cascade
Customizable powerline bar with chevron separators, plugin widgets (CPU, RAM, network, weather, battery), and multiple built-in palettes. The plugin powering your tmux2k slot — your Glacier is essentially a tighter, more deliberate version of this.

Dracula tmux

by dracula
powerline cascade · purple/pink
Signature Dracula palette: deep purple bg, pink/cyan accents, powerline arrow separators, wide right-side widget cluster (weather, battery, time, GPU).

Rosé Pine tmux

by rose-pine
muted · minimalist
Muted low-contrast pine/rose palette in Main, Moon, Dawn variants. Elegant minimal segments — quieter than catppuccin/dracula, more ambient than powerline. (README screenshots are signed-URL gated; logo shown.)

Tokyo Night tmux

by janoamaral
flat segments · neon accents
Deep navy "downtown Tokyo at night" bg with neon blue/violet accents. Flat segments with Spotify, weather, battery, Git status. Modern and clean — closer to your Atelier floating idea but darker.

tmux-power

by wfxr
heavy powerline · single accent
Bold powerline with arrow separators and saturated single-accent palettes (Gold, Moon, Coral, Everforest). Very high contrast — the "loudest" of the bunch. Aesthetic relative of your Bauhaus.

tmux-powerline (original)

by erikw
classic powerline · grandfather
The original / grandfather of all powerline-for-tmux setups: solid color blocks, sharp arrow separators, many segments (mail, mpd, weather, battery, hostname). What every modern theme is reacting to.

tmux-themepack

by jimeh
powerline · 9 colors × 3 layouts
Themepack of nine single-color powerline variants (blue, cyan, gray, green, magenta, orange, purple, red, yellow) in three layouts (default, block, double). The "swap a color via @plugin" experience.

tmux-nova

by o0th
segments framework · rectangular
Modern "segments framework" rather than a fixed theme — clean rectangular blocks with configurable left/right segments. Ships a Nord-style default that's popular in dotfile videos. Closest cousin to your Bauhaus plates.

tmux-gruvbox

by egel
retro · warm palette
Warm retro Gruvbox palette (mustard/orange on dark brown). Simple flat segments, no powerline. A staple in Neovim+Gruvbox dev setups. Spiritual cousin of your Bauhaus warmth.
